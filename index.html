Beagle

Beagle puppies are energetic, friendly companions with a keen sense of smell and a curious nature. Originally bred for hunting, they love to explore and follow scents. With their tricolored coats, floppy ears, and expressive eyes, beagles are irresistibly cute. They thrive in active households, needing regular exercise and mental stimulation. Loyal and affectionate, they make excellent family pets, forming strong bonds with their owners and bringing joy with their playful demeanor.
